An easy recipe for cookies for children

Make these cookies with your children, the cookies are both easy to make and very tasty!

ingredients

  • a cup of flour
  • 5 eggs
  • Half a cup of oil
  • A bag of baking powder
  • A cup and a half of sugar

preparation

  1. Put all the ingredients in a bowl and mix until the dough is smooth and soft.
  2. Roll out the dough and make shapes as you wish.
  3. Put the cookies in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 25 minutes.
